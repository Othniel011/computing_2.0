Consequences and Mentoring

Elyon Student Regulation:



This Student Regulations have been carefully prepared to help you understand the standards, values, and expectations that guide our school community. It outlines important school policies, academic guidelines, behavioral expectations, and student responsibilities designed to help you grow not only in knowledge but also in character and faith.



As a school that upholds the motto “To Be Victorious Leadersin Christ,” we believe that true success comes from a heart transformed by God and a life committed to His purpose. Through every subject, activity, and relationship, we pray you will be equipped to lead with integrity, serve with compassion, and shine His light wherever you go.



We encourage you to read this regulation carefully and refer to it throughout the year. Let it be a tool to guide your journey toward becoming the person God has called you to be both in school and beyond. May this year be filled with learning, growth, and God's abundant grace.
